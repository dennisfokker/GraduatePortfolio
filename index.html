About the assignment

The internship assignment was rather vague, due to the nature of how Enversed functions (generally only knows about projects a few weeks prior). Due to this, the assignment consists out of the products I made during my internship, whether they’re full games or extensions to their framework.

Keep in mind this was no conventional internship, as it consists out of multiple smaller products, rather than a single larger product.

About the company

Enversed Studios is a VR game development studio, creating applications with several different goals. Some of these goals are: visualisation, such as architectural visualisation (archviz) - entertainment, such as games for external clients or Enversed’s entertainment centre - Training, such as allowing employees to practise their tasks in VR, receiving valuable data that, for example, shows efficiency.
