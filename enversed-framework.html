hand collision

A system used to make a visual representation of your hand physical, whilst minimizing the chance of breaking the immersion of the game.

It provides extra visualisation for your real life hand position, which only becomes visible once your hand starts making illegal movements, like going into a wall.

HAND COLLISION

A system to make hands physical in the VR world. Could be used to prevent sequence-breaking through walls.

screenshot tool

There are scenarios where a user needs to capture the current visual state of the application. The screenshot tool is supposed to provide this service with certain ease-of-use features.

SCREENSHOT TOOL

A components that allow users to take screenshots from specific sources and preview what they would look like.
