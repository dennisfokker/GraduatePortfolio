proto game collection

The PROTO Invention Factory is a client who requested several games from Enversed, most of which I have worked on. All of these games exist in the same steampunk universe. Keep in mind that all games are intended to be of a somewhat-fixed length: they can’t take too long to play.

In these projects the “CONCEPT” heading specifies the game’s predetermined design, “DEVELOPMENT” lists sub-products I have worked on that are worth mentioning (generally fairly small). The design, development and process will be discussed within the “DEVELOPMENT” heading.

Keep in mind that for (almost) all of these games, I designed their score system, which is mostly the reason for the design tag. The realisation tag is due to the other created systems.

You can navigate to the product’s page by clicking the respective card (image).
