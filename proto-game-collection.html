About internship

proto back-end

The PROTO Invention Factory is a client who requested several games from Enversed, most of which I have worked on. All of these games exist in the same steampunk universe. Keep in mind that all games are intended to be of a somewhat-fixed length: they can’t take too long to play.

In these projects the “CONCEPT” heading specifies the game’s predetermined design, “DEVELOPMENT” lists sub-products I have worked on that are worth mentioning (generally fairly small). The design, development and process will be discussed within the “DEVELOPMENT” heading.

You can navigate to the product’s page by clicking the respective card (image).

AIRCYCLE

Aircycle is a game about cycling through a floating city with your airbike, delivering mail to the residents.

Follow the route through the city and try to reach all of the mail targets, whilst preventing to hit the environment.

AIRCYCLE

Cycle and fly your way through the city, delivering mail to the residents.

BALLOON ESCAPE

Balloon escape is a game about stealing your invention to prevent it being used for certain purposes.

Fly out of the “holding bay” and glide through an environmental obstacle course, staying below the lamps of their watch towers, to reach the escape boat.

BALLOON ESCAPE

Escape with your balloon, staying out of sight from the guards and making your way through treacherous terrain.

CAR INVENTOR RACE

Car inventor race is a game about racing your new car invention to the exposition, which you are late for.

Rush through the city in your car whilst swerving around traffic, avoiding pedestrians and crashing through pesky wooden blockades.

CAR INVENTOR RACE

Quickly drive your newest invention to the expo before it starts, evading hazards on the way.

INTRO EXPERIENCE

Intro experience is a game about you as an inventor, drawing out all the inventions: the ones you use in the other games.

Trace the guiding line and see the created doodle come to life, into something you’ll get to operate later!

INTRO EXPERIENCE

Draw out your newest ideas and see those ideas come to life into steampunk inventions.

LIGHT FACTORY

Light factory is a game about working in a brand new, state of the art, factory. Work effectively with your co-worker to make sure you meet the high demands.

Assemble a high-tech lamp piece by piece, but make sure you put on the right variation! Sadly you don’t have all the parts on your bench, so coordinate with your co-worker who has to perform what tasks.

LIGHT FACTORY

Work together with your coworker to keep up with the high demand for these new lamps.

STEAM RACERS

Steam racers is a game about racing in the brand new racing machines, finally making it possible to have organised races for prizes and amusement.

Step into one of the four steam racers, rev up the engine and race through the pipe system and over the slippery ice lake to be the first to finish all three laps.

STEAM RACERS

Race against up to three rivals in the newest racer. Evade the hazards and come out victorious.

SUBMARINE

Submarine is a game about taking a dive in the newest of submarines, get right into Atlantis and take home the precious treasure waiting down below.

Dive deep down to Atlantis with upto 2 other players, fighting off waves of automated defending robots to unlock the domes that hide the treasure inside.

SUBMARINE

Dive deep down to the city of Atlantis, where you fight their automated defenses and steal the lost treasure.

TRAIN HEIST

Train heist is a game about defending a new inventions which is in the process of being stolen by hijackers.

Team up with your friend and shoot your waves through the bandits, making your way through the train to prevent them from taking away the invention.

TRAIN HEIST

Shoot your way through the baddies on the train to prevent them from stealing the invention on the other side.
